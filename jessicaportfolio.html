Special Coagulation

Identified deficiencies in product line portfolio valuation processes. Single product line analysis identified certain special coagulation tests negatively impacting key profitability metrics. However, there was no formal quantitative method in place to recognize the impact that these tests had on MCL’s ability to capture higher margin tests and positive impact to overall MCL portfolio. Results of analysis were used to develop future metrics to assess special coagulation product line and overall MCL portfolio value.

Neurogenetics

Market research and analysis identified pharmaceutical partnerships as a non-traditional method to increase neurogenetic product line portfolio value and mitigate losses from poorly reimbursed genetic testing. Creative insights also challenged legacy thinking about MCL’s value proposition by uncovering the need for a paradigm shift; exposed gaps in the market’s perception about MCL’s superior quality that generated a significant pivot in product strategy.
